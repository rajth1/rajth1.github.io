About

Quantitative thinker,
curious by nature.

I'm Raj Thakur, a Bachelor's student in Mathematical Sciences with a focus on actuarial science, data analysis, and quantitative modelling. I'm a member of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA), and have passed my first two professional exams — CS1 (Actuarial Statistics) and CM1 (Actuarial Mathematics for Finance and Insurance).

My interests span the intersection of mathematics, risk, and intelligent systems — from classical statistical inference to modern machine learning and AI. I'm drawn to work that demands rigorous reasoning: research, quantitative finance, risk modelling, and data science.

Beyond the technical, I value clear communication, collaborative leadership, and the ability to translate complex models into actionable insight.
